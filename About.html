About Us

Since the founding of the Krusty Krab in 1999, we have been committed to serving our commuinty with delicious food you can't find anywhere else.

The Krusty Krab is a family-owned business that believes in the power of the Krabby Patty to change the world. Despite our small team of employees, we pride ourselves on our reasonable prices and exceptional service.
